Algorithms are the invisible engines of our digital world. But beyond their utility, there is an inherent beauty in their design.

## Elegance in Logic

A well-designed algorithm is like a well-written poem. It is concise, powerful, and efficient.

- **QuickSort**: A masterclass in recursion.
- **Dijkstra's**: Elegance in shortest-path finding.
- **Binary Search**: Simplicity at its finest.

In future posts, we will explore each of these in detail.
