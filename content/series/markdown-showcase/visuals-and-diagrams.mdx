Visualizing information is key to understanding. Amytis integrates Mermaid.js for diagrams and handles images with ease.

## Mermaid Diagrams

You can write diagrams directly in your markdown:

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[Idea] --> B{Is it good?}
    B -- Yes --> C[Plant it]
    B -- No --> D[Discard]
    C --> E[Grow]
    E --> F[Evergreen]
```
